OneAccess Can Help Keep Facilities Safe & Healthy During Recent COVID-19 Surges

Posted by on 2023-10-02

While many say we're living in post-pandemic times as quarantines, curfews and mask mandates have been lifted, recent months have seen a surge in COVID-19 cases as preventative measures have eased up. As cold and flu season is hitting North America, keeping your staff, guests and facilities clean, safe and healthy is key. 

That's where OneAccess can help.

OneAccess is your comprehensive visitor screening tool that simplifies the registration process  for staff, visitors, residents and contractors upon entry and exit. With the added convenience of touchless entry through QR codes or our kiosk app on an iPad, you can rest assured that your facility's security and health standards are maintained. 

OneAccess is especially useful for senior care facilities that need to track who is coming in and out of their building, especially with the prospect of outbreaks.
